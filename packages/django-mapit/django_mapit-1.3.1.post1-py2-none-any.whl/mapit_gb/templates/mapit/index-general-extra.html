The main difference in generation 12 is that it contains the pre-2010 England and Wales Parliamentary constituencies; generation 14 contains the pre-2011 Scottish Parliament constituencies. Only current Northern Ireland constituencies are present; if you need pre-2010 UK Parliament NI constituencies, or pre-2011 Northern Ireland Assembly constituencies, you can work it out from the wards and the 2003 SNAC file in the data/ directory.
